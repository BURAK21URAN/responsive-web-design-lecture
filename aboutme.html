Burak Uran

I was born in 2002 in Soma, a district of Manisa, Turkey. In 2008, my family and I moved to Silvan, a district in Diyarbakır, where I completed the first grade of primary school. Afterward, in 2010, we moved to Hatay. I completed my primary education in İskenderun, a district of Hatay.

Following primary school, I continued my education in Dörtyol, Hatay, and completed middle school there. In 2016, my family and I moved to Akşehir, a district in Konya. I graduated from Akşehir Science High School, where I completed my high school education.

In 2021, I moved to Ankara to pursue my university studies. Currently, I am studying Software Engineering at Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University. The diverse experiences I gained from living in different cities have significantly contributed to my personal and professional growth. I continue to develop myself in the field of software engineering.
